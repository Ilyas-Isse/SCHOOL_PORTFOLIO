Our Story

A Legacy Built on Passion & Purpose

Founded in 1985 by a visionary group of educators, Westridge Academy began as a small community school with a bold idea: that every child deserves a world-class education. Over four decades, that vision grew into a nationally recognized institution of excellence.

Today our 50-acre campus hosts state-of-the-art science labs, an Olympic-standard sports complex, a 400-seat performing arts theatre, and one of the finest libraries in the region. Yet our greatest asset remains our people — teachers who inspire, students who dare to dream, and a community that lifts each other up.

Over 15,000 alumni have gone on to lead corporations, advance science, create art, and serve communities around the globe. We are immensely proud of each one.

Our Journey

40 Years of Milestones

1985

Academy Founded

Westridge Academy opens its doors with 120 students and 12 dedicated teachers in a modest campus.

1993

STEM Center Opens

State-of-the-art science and technology center inaugurated, attracting students from across the region.

2002

National #1 Ranking

Westridge earns its first national #1 ranking by the National Education Board — a title held for 12 consecutive years.

2010

Sports Complex & Arts Theatre

New Olympic-standard athletic facilities and a 400-seat performing arts theatre completed.

2020

Digital & AI Curriculum

Launched pioneering AI & machine learning curriculum, placing Westridge at the forefront of 21st-century education.

2026

40th Anniversary

Celebrating four decades of excellence with 15,000+ alumni, 40+ programs, and a global impact that continues to grow.

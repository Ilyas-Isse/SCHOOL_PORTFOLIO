Who We Are

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1985

Founded with a vision to transform education, Westridge Academy has grown from a modest institution into one of the nation's most distinguished schools. Our campus spans over 50 acres of state-of-the-art facilities.

We believe that education is more than academics — it's about building character, developing critical thinking, and preparing students to thrive in an ever-changing world.
